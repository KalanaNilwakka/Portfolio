'Sipsara' Educational Charity 2023 - Phase 01

I am thrilled to have played a key role in the successful execution of the Educational Charity Event 2023 - Phase 01, organized by the Catholic Students' Movement at the University of Kelaniya.
This impactful initiative took place on November 24th at 'The Lady of Miracle Maha Vidyalaya, Negombo.' The event aimed to make a meaningful contribution to education, focusing on creating positive changes in the lives of students. Through collaborative efforts and community engagement, we provided valuable resources and support to enhance the educational experience at 'The Lady of Miracle Maha Vidyalaya.'
It was a day filled with inspiration, learning, and the joy of giving back to the community. I am proud to have been part of a team dedicated to making a difference in the education sector, fostering a spirit of generosity, and embodying the values of service and compassion. Looking forward to more opportunities to contribute to meaningful causes and create a positive impact in the community.

JumborIEEE'23 - Unravel 2.0

I am thrilled to have been part of the organizing committee for JumborIEEE'23 - Unravel 2.0, a collaborative event by the IEEE chapters of SLIIT, UOK, and UCSC. This was held on 10th of December at University of Colombo, School of Computing premises. Beyond the invaluable learning experience in event planning and timeline management, the journey was incredibly enjoyable. The event's emphasis on fun was infectious, and being part of the committee allowed me not only to contribute to its success but also to partake in the excitement. This unique blend of learning and fun made my role even more fulfilling, creating lasting memories and reinforcing the importance of teamwork in creating a vibrant and engaging event.
